GEAR SYSTEMS

Gears are toothed mechanical components that transmit rotational force (torque) and change speed ratios between shafts. A gear pair with a 2:1 ratio doubles torque while halving speed — the fundamental trade-off in mechanical power transmission.

  • ⚙ Spur, Helical, Bevel & Worm types
  • ⚙ Used in gearboxes, watches, mills
  • ⚙ Efficiency up to 99% in precision sets

IC ENGINE

Internal Combustion Engines convert chemical energy of fuel into mechanical work through controlled explosions inside cylinders. The 4-stroke Otto cycle — intake, compression, power, exhaust — is the backbone of automotive propulsion.

  • 🔥 4-stroke & 2-stroke variants
  • 🔥 Petrol, Diesel, CNG fuel types
  • 🔥 Thermal efficiency ~25-40%

CNC MACHINES

Computer Numerical Control machines execute pre-programmed sequences of machining operations with micron-level precision. G-code instructions direct multi-axis movements of cutting tools to shape metals, plastics, and composites.

  • 🔩 Tolerances of ±0.001 mm
  • 🔩 3, 4 and 5-axis configurations
  • 🔩 Used in aerospace, auto & medical

ROBOTICS

Industrial robots combine mechanical linkages, actuators, sensors, and control algorithms to automate complex tasks. Forward and inverse kinematics govern how joint angles translate to end-effector position in 3D workspace.

  • 🤖 6-DOF articulated arms
  • 🤖 Welding, painting, assembly tasks
  • 🤖 Position accuracy < 0.05 mm

HYDRAULICS

Hydraulic systems transmit power via pressurized fluid (Pascal's Law). A small input force on a small piston generates a large output force on a larger piston — the principle behind excavators, brakes, and industrial presses.

  • 💧 Pressures up to 700 bar
  • 💧 Excavators, cranes, aircraft actuators
  • 💧 Power density far exceeds electric motors

THERMODYNAMICS

Thermodynamics governs energy conversion and transfer in mechanical systems. The four laws establish principles of heat, work, and entropy — underpinning power cycles like Rankine (steam turbines) and Brayton (jet engines).

  • 🌡 Carnot efficiency limits all heat engines
  • 🌡 Rankine cycle powers 80% of global electricity
  • 🌡 Entropy always increases in real processes
